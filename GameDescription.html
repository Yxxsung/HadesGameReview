Plot/Lore *SPOILERS*

In the videogame Hades, you play as the son of Hades, Zagreus. Zagreus is trying to escape the underworld to find his birth mother, having just discovered Nix is not his real mother. While playing as Zagreus, you fight your way through the three levels of the underworld; Tartarus, Asphodel, and Aluyssium before fighting Hades himself on the journey to find his mother, Persephone
