Hades The Video Game UNOFFICIAL

Game Details and Description

Plot/Lore *SPOILERS*

In the videogame Hades, you play as the son of Hades, Zagreus. Zagreus is trying to escape the underworld to find his birth mother, having just discovered Nix is not his real mother. While playing as Zagreus, you fight your way through the three levels of the underworld; Tartarus, Asphodel, and Eluysium before fighting Hades himself on the journey to find his mother, Persephone.
In each of the three levels you encounter various enemies of different strengths including two bosses per level.

  • Tartarus: Alecto, Megaera, and Tisiphone (the Furies)
  • Asphodel: Bone Hydra
  • Elysium: Theseus and Asterius
