Hades The Video Game UNOFFICIAL

Game Details and Description

Plot/Lore *SPOILERS*

In the videogame Hades, you play as the son of Hades, Zagreus. Zagreus is trying to escape the underworld to find his birth mother, having just discovered Nix is not his real mother.
While playing as Zagreus, you fight your way through the three levels of the underworld; Tartarus, Asphodel, and Eluysium before fighting Hades himself on the journey to find his mother, Persephone.
In each of the three levels you encounter various enemies of different strengths including two bosses per level.

  • Tartarus: The Inferno Bomber, along with Alecto, Megaera, and Tisiphone (the Furies)
  • Asphodel: Bone Hydra
  • Elysium: Theseus and Asterius


Besides these main bosses, there are a lot of other bosses, all of them representing souls that have lived
as mortals and become these creatures once they died and were sorted into their roles in teh underworld.
